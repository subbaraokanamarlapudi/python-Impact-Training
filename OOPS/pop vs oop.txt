POP

* It is a procedural oriented language.
* Programs are divided into functions.
* Top-down approach.
* No access specifiers.
* No data security.
* No overloading concepts.
EX: C , VB script , forton , pascal.

OOP

* It is a object oriented programming.
* Programs are divided into objects.
* Bottom-up approach.
* Provide access specifiers.
* Data security.
* functions overloading , method overriding , operator overloading.

EX : Java , c++ , python
